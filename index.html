Case Study 02 — Revenue Attribution Analysis

Isolating a 23% revenue decline to one segment, one window, one fix.

Revenue was down 23% across the portfolio and leadership had a dozen theories. I built a Python and SQL attribution model across 1M+ CRM records to trace the decline. It was isolated to a single customer segment over a 2-week window. The attribution analysis then revealed that 2 channels were driving 78% of revenue at 3.5x ROAS while 3 channels consumed 22% of budget with near-zero return.

The recommendation changed how the team allocated budget the following quarter. ROI improved. That is what this work is for.


Tools: Python · SQL · Scikit-learn · Tableau · Looker · CRM data · SciPy A/B testing
