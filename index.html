Case 02 / 2022–2024Revenue Attribution — CRM Analytics — Campaign Optimization
The Brief
Revenue down 23%. Leadership has a dozen theories. Find the real answer.
The Finding
Built a Python and SQL attribution model across 1M+ CRM records. Isolated the decline to a single customer segment over a two-week window. Then identified that 2 channels drove 78% of revenue at 3.5x ROAS while 3 others burned 22% of budget at under 1.2x.
The Impact
Budget reallocated. Campaign strategy shifted. ROI improved. This is what data-backed marketing looks like — not a dashboard, but a decision that changes how the team operates.
